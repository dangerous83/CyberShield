[ GUIDELINES · 10 ]

Use it right.

A brand only works when it's protected. Three rules to live by — and three to never break.

Do

Maintain clear space

Keep at least the height of the shield clear on every side.

Use approved backgrounds

White, off-white, and Cyber Black only — anything else compromises legibility.

Scale proportionally

Always preserve the original aspect ratio. Resize from the corner — never the edge.

Don't

Don't stretch or distort

The geometry is engineered — never compress or expand the mark.

Don't recolor outside the system

Only the approved palette. The mark is not a canvas for off-brand color.

Don't tilt or rotate

The shield always stands upright. Stability is a brand attribute.
